COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before working with us.

How fast do you deliver permit packages?+
Our standard turnaround is 24–48 hours for most permit packages. Rush delivery (same-day) is available for urgent projects. We've never missed a committed deadline.
Do you serve all 50 US states?+
Yes. We provide services across all 50 US states, Canada-wide, and throughout the United Kingdom — with full local code compliance and AHJ familiarity for each region.
What information do I need to get started?+
For permit packages: basic project details like site address, system size, and equipment specs. For CAD/BIM work: your existing drawings or design brief. We'll guide you through the rest.
Do you offer revisions if the AHJ requests changes?+
Absolutely. We stand behind every deliverable and provide free revisions on permit packages if the Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ) requests corrections. Your approval is our goal.
Can you handle large volumes of projects simultaneously?+
Yes. We routinely process high volumes for large installers, contractors, and national firms. We scale with your pipeline — bulk pricing is available for ongoing partners.
